## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. **Sign Up:**
   - Visit the Store Master website
   - Click **"Get Started"** or **"Sign Up"**
   - Enter your email address and create a password
   - Verify your email address

2. **Set Up Your Organization:**
   - Enter your business name
   - Choose a unique organization identifier (slug)
   - Provide business contact information
   - Click **"Create Organization"**

3. **Select Your Plan:**
   - Review available subscription plans
   - Choose the plan that fits your business needs
   - Start with a free trial or select a paid plan
   - Complete payment if selecting a paid plan

4. **Welcome Tour:**
   - Follow the guided tour to learn key features
   - Set up your first products and categories
   - Explore the dashboard

### Joining an Existing Organization

If you've been invited to join an organization:

1. **Check Your Email:**
   - Look for an invitation email from Store Master
   - Click the invitation link

2. **Accept Invitation:**
   - Review the organization details
   - Click **"Accept Invitation"**
   - Sign in or create an account if needed

3. **Access the System:**
   - You'll be automatically added to the organization
   - Your role and permissions are set by the organization owner
   - Start using the system based on your assigned role

## Team Management

### Understanding Roles

Store Master has three user roles:

**Owner:**
- Full access to all features
- Manage subscriptions and billing
- Add and remove team members
- Assign roles and permissions
- Access all data and settings

**Admin:**
- Manage inventory and products
- Process sales and invoices
- Manage customers
- View reports
- Cannot manage billing or remove owner

**Member:**
- Process sales at point of sale
- View products and inventory
- Create invoices
- Limited access to reports
- Cannot modify settings

### Inviting Team Members

**Steps to invite:**

1. **Navigate to Team:**
   - Click your profile icon
   - Select **"Team Members"**
   - Or go to Settings → Team

2. **Send Invitation:**
   - Click **"Invite Member"**
   - Enter the person's email address
   - Select their role (Admin or Member)
   - Add a personal message (optional)
   - Click **"Send Invitation"**

3. **Track Invitations:**
   - View pending invitations
   - Resend invitations if needed
   - Cancel invitations before acceptance

**Important Notes:**
- Invitations expire after 7 days
- Each plan has a maximum number of users
- Upgrade your plan to add more team members

### Managing Team Members

**Viewing Team Members:**
- See all active members
- View their roles and permissions
- Check when they joined
- See last login time

**Changing Roles:**
1. Find the team member
2. Click **"Edit"** or the role dropdown
3. Select new role
4. Confirm the change

**Removing Team Members:**
1. Find the team member
2. Click **"Remove"** or the menu icon
3. Confirm removal
4. They lose access immediately

**Important:** Removed members cannot access organization data but their historical actions remain in the system.

### Team Collaboration Best Practices

1. **Assign Appropriate Roles:**
   - Give users the minimum permissions they need
   - Promote to Admin only when necessary
   - Keep Owner role limited

2. **Regular Review:**
   - Review team members quarterly
   - Remove inactive users
   - Update roles as responsibilities change

3. **Communication:**
   - Inform team of their role and responsibilities
   - Provide training on system features
   - Set expectations for data entry

4. **Security:**
   - Encourage strong passwords
   - Remove access for departing employees immediately
   - Monitor unusual activity

## Billing and Payments

### Payment Methods

Store Master uses **Flutterwave** for secure payment processing.

**Accepted Payment Methods:**
- Debit Cards (Mastercard, Visa, Verve)
- Bank Transfers
- USSD
- Bank Accounts
- Mobile Money

### Making a Payment

**For New Subscriptions:**
1. Select your desired plan
2. Choose billing cycle (monthly/yearly)
3. Click **"Subscribe"**
4. You'll be redirected to Flutterwave
5. Complete payment using your preferred method
6. Return to Store Master
7. Your subscription activates immediately

**For Renewals:**
- Payments process automatically on renewal date
- You'll receive a reminder 7 days before
- Ensure your payment method is valid
- Check your email for payment confirmation

### Viewing Billing History

**To access:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Subscription**
2. Click **"Billing History"**
3. View all past payments:
   - Date and amount
   - Payment method
   - Invoice number
   - Receipt (download)

**Download Receipts:**
- Click on any payment
- Click **"Download Receipt"**
- Save for your records

### Upgrading Your Plan

**When to upgrade:**
- Approaching user limit
- Need more products
- Require additional features
- Want priority support

**Steps to upgrade:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Subscription**
2. Click **"Upgrade Plan"**
3. Select new plan
4. Review prorated charges
5. Complete payment
6. New features available immediately

**Prorated Billing:**
- You only pay for the remaining time in your billing cycle
- Example: Upgrade halfway through month = 50% of price difference
- Credit applied automatically

### Downgrading Your Plan

**Steps to downgrade:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Subscription**
2. Click **"Change Plan"**
3. Select lower tier plan
4. Review what you'll lose
5. Confirm downgrade

**Important Notes:**
- Downgrade takes effect at end of current billing period
- You keep current features until then
- Ensure you're within new plan limits
- No refund for unused time

**Before downgrading, check:**
- Current number of team members vs. new limit
- Current number of products vs. new limit
- Monthly invoice volume vs. new limit
- Storage usage vs. new limit

### Cancelling Your Subscription

**Steps to cancel:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Subscription**
2. Click **"Cancel Subscription"**
3. Select cancellation reason (helps us improve)
4. Choose when to cancel:
   - **End of billing period**: Keep access until paid period ends
   - **Immediately**: Lose access now (no refund)
5. Confirm cancellation

**What happens after cancellation:**
- Access continues until period end (if selected)
- No further charges
- Data preserved for 30 days
- Can reactivate anytime
- Export your data before deletion

### Failed Payments

**If a payment fails:**
1. You'll receive an email notification
2. Grace period begins (7 days)
3. Update your payment method
4. Retry payment manually
5. Contact support if issues persist

**During grace period:**
- Full access continues
- Update payment method in Settings
- Retry payment
- Contact support for help

**After grace period:**
- Access restricted to read-only
- Cannot create new data
- Can view existing data
- Must pay to restore full access

### Refund Policy

**Refunds are available for:**
- Duplicate charges
- Technical errors
- Service not delivered

**Refunds are NOT available for:**
- Change of mind
- Unused time (monthly plans)
- Downgrade requests

**To request a refund:**
1. Contact support@storemaster.com
2. Provide transaction details
3. Explain the reason
4. Allow 5-7 business days for processing

## Account Settings

### Profile Settings

**Update your profile:**
1. Click your profile icon
2. Select **"Profile Settings"**
3. Update:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Phone number
   - Profile picture
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

### Organization Settings

**Update organization details:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Organization**
2. Update:
   - Business name
   - Contact information
   - Business address
   - Logo
3. Click **"Save"**

**Note:** Only Owners can modify organization settings.

### Security Settings

**Change Password:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **"Change Password"**
3. Enter current password
4. Enter new password
5. Confirm new password
6. Click **"Update Password"**

**Enable Two-Factor Authentication (if available):**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Security**
2. Click **"Enable 2FA"**
3. Scan QR code with authenticator app
4. Enter verification code
5. Save backup codes

### Notification Preferences

**Manage notifications:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Choose notification types:
   - Email notifications
   - In-app notifications
   - SMS notifications (if available)
3. Select what to be notified about:
   - Billing and payments
   - Team changes
   - System updates
   - Low stock alerts
4. Click **"Save Preferences"**

### Data Export

**Export your data:**
1. Go to **Settings** → **Data**
2. Click **"Export Data"**
3. Select data types:
   - Products
   - Customers
   - Invoices
   - Transactions
4. Choose format (CSV, Excel)
5. Click **"Export"**
6. Download when ready

**Use cases:**
- Backup your data
- Migrate to another system
- Analyze in external tools
- Compliance requirements

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Can I try Store Master before paying?**
A: Yes! The Free plan lets you explore the platform with basic features. You can upgrade anytime.

**Q: Can I change my plan later?**
A: Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from the Subscription settings.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes. We use industry-standard encryption, secure data centers, and regular backups. Your data is isolated from other organizations.

**Q: Can I use Store Master offline?**
A: Yes! Store Master works offline and syncs when you're back online. Perfect for areas with unreliable internet.

**Q: Do you offer training?**
A: We provide comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and email support. Enterprise customers get dedicated onboarding.

### Subscription Questions

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept all major debit cards, bank transfers, USSD, and mobile money through Flutterwave.

**Q: Can I pay annually?**
A: Yes! Annual billing saves you up to 20% compared to monthly billing.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my plan limits?**
A: You'll see a notification prompting you to upgrade. Some operations may be restricted until you upgrade or reduce usage.

**Q: Can I get a refund if I cancel?**
A: Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable. Annual subscriptions may be eligible for prorated refunds within 30 days.

**Q: Do you offer discounts?**
A: We offer discounts for annual billing, non-profits, and educational institutions. Contact sales for details.

### Team Questions

**Q: How many users can I have?**
A: It depends on your plan:
- Free: 2 users
- Basic: 5 users
- Professional: 15 users
- Enterprise: Unlimited

**Q: Can team members have different permissions?**
A: Yes. You can assign Owner, Admin, or Member roles with different permission levels.

**Q: What happens when a team member leaves?**
A: Remove them from your team. They lose access immediately, but their historical data remains.

**Q: Can I transfer ownership?**
A: Yes. Contact support to transfer ownership to another team member.

### Billing Questions

**Q: When will I be charged?**
A: You're charged immediately when subscribing, then on the same date each billing cycle.

**Q: What if my payment fails?**
A: You'll get a 7-day grace period to update your payment method and retry.

**Q: Can I get an invoice for my payments?**
A: Yes. Download invoices from your Billing History in Subscription settings.

**Q: Do prices include VAT?**
A: Prices are shown in Nigerian Naira. VAT may apply based on your location.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Store Master works on Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions).

**Q: Can I use Store Master on mobile?**
A: Yes! The interface is responsive and works on tablets and phones.

**Q: Do you have a mobile app?**
A: Currently, we offer a web application that works on mobile browsers. Native apps are coming soon.

**Q: Can I integrate with other tools?**
A: Professional and Enterprise plans include API access for custom integrations.

**Q: What if I need help?**
A: Contact support@storemaster.com. Response times vary by plan:
- Free/Basic: 48 hours
- Professional: 24 hours
- Enterprise: 4 hours

### Data Questions

**Q: Who owns my data?**
A: You do! Your data belongs to you and your organization.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes. Export your data anytime in CSV or Excel format.

**Q: How long do you keep my data after cancellation?**
A: We keep your data for 30 days after cancellation, then permanently delete it.

**Q: Is my data backed up?**
A: Yes. We perform daily backups and can restore data if needed.

**Q: Can other organizations see my data?**
A: No. Your data is completely isolated and only accessible to your team members.

## Appendix

### Glossary

- **Organization**: Your business entity in Store Master
- **Subscription**: Your paid plan and billing arrangement
- **Team Member**: A user who belongs to your organization
- **Owner**: The primary administrator with full access
- **Admin**: A user with management permissions
- **Member**: A user with basic access permissions
- **Flutterwave**: Our payment processing partner
- **RLS**: Row-Level Security - ensures data isolation
- **Prorated**: Proportional billing based on time used

### Keyboard Shortcuts

- `Ctrl/Cmd + K`: Quick search
- `Ctrl/Cmd + N`: New product/invoice (context-dependent)
- `Ctrl/Cmd + S`: Save current form
- `Esc`: Close dialog/modal
- `/`: Focus search bar

**Minimum:**
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection (for sync)
- 2 GB RAM
- 1024x768 screen resolution

**Recommended:**
- Latest browser version
- Stable internet connection
- 4 GB RAM
- 1920x1080 screen resolution
